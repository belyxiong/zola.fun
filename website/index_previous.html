WHAT IS ZOLA?

ZOLA is a decentralized charity lottery game platform based on Ethereum network, it is absolute transparent and fair, player use ZOLA game token to make a bet, one bet come with a 3, 4 or 6 digital number, at the end of each game period, platform will get 3, 4 or 6 digital number from the stock market as winning number, if your number exactly matches with the winning number then you will win, as the stock market is huge, no one can control it, and when the stock market closes, everyone knows what the winning number is and how it comes, so it is absolute transparent and fair to all players. Before play the game, player will need to get ZOLA game token (ZGT), the offical way to get is exchange using ETH on ZolaX, ZolaX will keep a exchange platform where player can buy and sale the ZOLA game token, the exchange rate will be fixed for ever.



As a charity lottery, in every game period (normally every workday), we will donate 10% of all bet tokens or equal ETHs to selected charity organizations in world wide, ZOLA share token holders will decide which charity organizations to receive the donations by voting, charity organizations can contact us for details of listing in our system.

HOW ZOLA WORKS

ZOLA is built based on Ethereum network, user will need to get ZOLA game token(ZGT) before play, ZOLA game token can be got by exchanging using ETH, or you can buy it from other users, ZOLA will keep a exchange platform where player can buy and sale ZOLA game tokens, the exchange rate will be fixed for ever. We will run the lottery draw on every business day in different country regions (US and China at the first stage), every business day from 6:00AM-2:30PM is the time to make bet, after 2:30PM you cannot participate today's lottery game. When you make a bet, you will need to choose a 4 or 6 digital numbers from 0-9, and order them as your wish, numbers can be same on different positions, after the stock market closed, we will pick the 3 most important stock index from the region(for US zone, we will choose Nasdaq Composite, Dow Jones Average and S&P 500, for China zone, we will select SH000001, SZ399001 and SH000300), select 2 digital numbers after the decimal point from each of them to compose the winning number, we will have three types game, one is guess 3 numbers, one is guess 4 numbers and another is guess 6 numbers, if your bet has the exactly same 6 digital numbers, then you are one winner, different from PowerBall game, we will have 1 level prize, only when all of your number match exactly with the winning number, you will be winner, otherwise you cannot win, see more details please check FAQ.

Why it is absolute transparent and fair?

Whenever you enter our website you will see how many and what numbers has been placed by other players, and when you select your number, you will know how many ZOLA game tokens you can win if you match different numbers. The most important thing is compare to other lotter games, ZOLA will pick the famous stock indexes to compose the winner number, so everyboday knows how the number comes and why the numbers come, unlike other lottery games, you never know how the winning number comes and why, but on ZOLA you will know, so it is absolute transparent and fair.

FAQ'S

  • What is ZOLA?

    ZOLA is an Ethereum based lottery game platform, allow player to use ZOLA game tokens (ZGT) to make bet and to win ZOLA game tokens.

  • What is the main difference between ZOLA and other lottery game platform?

    ZOLA is an absolute transparent and fair lottery game platform, it let player knows how many the player can win when make a bet and player also knows how the winning number comes and why the winning number is that, it is totally transparent.

  • What needed to play on ZOLA?

    Player need to get ZOLA game tokens to play on ZOLA, ZOLA game tokens will be distributed to public, player can use ETH to exchange ZOLA game tokens.

  • What does ZOLA stand for?

    ZOLA comes from ZO and LA, ZO is similiar to got, win in Chinese, LA means happy,pleasure.

  • How to play?

    Once you get ZOLA game token, you can participate in the game on business day, you need choose a number with 3, 4, or 6 integer digitals, like 123, 4565 or 434532, you can choose how many times to bet on the number, by default it is one.

  • How can I win?

    If your bet number is exactly same as the winning number then you will win. We will have 3 types games, guess 3/4/6 digital number, for any type game, your bet number should exactly match with the winning number to be a winner, otherwise you cannot win.

  • How does the winning number come?

    On every business day, after the stock closed, ZOLA platform will pick 3 important indexes from the market, for US zone, it will select Nasdaq Composite, Dow Jones Average and S & P 500 (the order is fixed), and use the digital numbers after the decimal point from each of them to compose the winning number, for example:
    Nasdaq Composite is : 4233.3434
    Dow Jones Average is : 2325.9232
    S & P 500 is : 8753.3543

    For guess 3 digital number game, the winning number is composed by the first digital after decimal point of each index: 3 + 9 + 8 => 398

    For guess 4 digital number game, the winning number is composed by the first 2 digital number after decimal of Nasdaq Composite and Dow Jones Average : 34 + 92 => 3492

    For guess 6 digital number game, the winning number is composed by the first 2 digital number after decimal of all three indexes : 34 + 92 + 35=> 349235
