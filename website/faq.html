FAQs


What is ZOLA?

ZOLA is an Ethereum network based lottery game platform, allow player to use ZOLA game tokens (ZGT) to make bet and to win ZOLA tokens.


What is the main difference between ZOLA and other lottery game platform?

ZOLA is an absolute transparent and fair lottery game platform, it let player knows how many the player can win when make a bet and player also knows how the winning number comes and why the winning number is that, it is totally transparent and fair.


What needed to play on ZOLA?

Player need to get ZOLA game tokens(ZGT) to play on ZOLA, ZOLA game tokens will be distributed to public, player can use ETH to exchange ZOLA game tokens.


What does ZOLA stand for?

ZOLA comes from ZO and LA, ZO is similiar to got, win in Chinese, LA means happy,pleased.


How to play?

Once you get ZOLA game token(ZGT), you can participate in the game on business day, you need choose a number with 3, 4, or 6 integer digitals, like 123, 4565 or 434532, you can choose how many times to bet on the number, by default it is one.


How can I win?

If your bet number is exactly same as the winning number then you will win. We will have 3 types games, guess 3/4/6 digital number, for any type game, your bet number should exactly match with the winning number to be a winner, otherwise you cannot win.


How does the winning number come?

On every business day, after the stock closed, ZOLA platform will pick 3 important indexes from the market, for US zone, it will select Dow Jones Average, S & P 500 and Nasdaq Composite (the order will be fixed later), and use the digital numbers after the decimal point from each of them to compose the winning number, for example:
Dow Jones Average is : 21709.34
S & P 500 is : 2477.83
Nasdaq Composite is : 6422.75

For guess 3 digital number game, the winning number is composed by the first digital after decimal point of each index: 3 + 8 + 7 => 387


For guess 4 digital number game, the winning number is composed by the first 2 digital number after decimal of S & P 500 and Dow Jones Average : 34 + 83 => 3483


For guess 6 digital number game, the winning number is composed by the first 2 digital number after decimal of all three indexes : 34 + 83 + 75=> 348375



How many game tokens can I win?

For different type game, all winners bet on that game will share the tokens in that game pool based on how many times bet that.
